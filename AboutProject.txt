This is my Project of Masters.

Project is about Ordering Customizable Shirts Online.

Where User can create Custome shirt with his idea in mind and send request to near by Tailors.

Tailors can give him a offer like, i can made you this type of shirt in this budget if user likes it he accept the offer.

And Tailors have option where they can create their Custome shirt in system and add them to their Collection.

Near by Users can see their Collection and if they like the design of a shirt they simply give order to a tailor of that particular design with their measurments.
